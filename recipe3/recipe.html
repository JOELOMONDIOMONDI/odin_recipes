Christmas Eve Beef Stew

This is a family tradition for Christmas Eve!
Serve with a green salad and a loaf of warm bread. It can also be made in a slow cooker.

Ingridients

  • 2 ½ pounds beef stew, diced into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 (28 ounce) can stewed tomatoes, with juice
  • 1 cup chopped cellery
  • 4 carrots sliced
  • 3 potatoes, cubed
  • 3 onions chopped
  • 3 ½ tablespoon tapiaco
  • 2 cubes beef bouillon
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried marjoram
  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen green peas, thawed

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C).
  2. Place beef, tomatoes, celery, carrots, potatoes,
    onions, and tapioca into a Dutch oven.
    Season with beef bouillon, thyme, rosemary, and marjoram; stir in red wine.
    Place the lid on the Dutch oven.
  3. Bake for 5 to 6 hours in the preheated oven. Add peas during last half hour of cooking.
